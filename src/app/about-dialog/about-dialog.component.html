About KeybindPraccer

I recently started playing World of Warcraft and Dota2. I wanted to develop a fast way to practice my keybinds and improve my mechanical skill.

Click options to add/delete keybind.

Use an alias to display the key or what the key represent in game example Alias: Attack, Key: e
